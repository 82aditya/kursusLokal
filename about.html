About SkillSpotter

SkillSpotter is a community-driven platform that helps people discover and share offline skill classes in their local area. Our goal is to empower local instructors and learners to connect without needing to rely on virtual or remote setups.

Whether you're passionate about cooking, crafting, technology, or teaching a language, we want to help you build real connections in the real world.
